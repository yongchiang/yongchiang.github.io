About me

I’m currently an associate professor in Northwest Polytechnical University. I’m interested in Video Understanding and Human Action Understanding.

Previously, I obtained my PhD degree at mmlab, the Chinese University of Hong Kong, where I am supervised by Prof.Dahua Lin. I obtained my Bachelor degree at Peking University, where I worked with Prof.Hongbing Zha.

In my normal life, I like peoms, cooking and aerobics!

Research

Northwestern Polytechnical University - Laboratory Research Directions
